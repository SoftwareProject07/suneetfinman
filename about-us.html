About Our Company

Suneet Finman Pvt. Ltd. is in the operations from the year 1995. Suneet Finman Pvt Ltd is registered with Registrar of Companies Delhi & Haryana vide CIN NO. U67120DL1995PTC072870 & is also registered u/s 45IA of RBI Act with Reserve Bank of India. vide registration No B-14.02705 Dated 21/09/2002 as Non-Deposit taking Company The registered office of the company is situated at C-83-A, F/F, Gali No.-4, Ganesh Nagar Complex, Pandav Nagar,Near Mother Dairy, Delhi 110092.

We are experienced and early adopters of finance market after globalization came to India. Working as NBFC is rewarding as well as a challenge worth taking on and we have been at it for many years.

The company is focused to grow multi fold in the coming years focused in the Delhi NCR. Strategy is to grow MSME loans, new and used four wheeler, two wheeler, Loan Against Property,E-Rickshaw Loan and Secured Business loans. The company has a fabulous track record of growth and has branches and collection centers in Delhi NCR. Suneet Finman Pvt Ltd is driven to provide lending to customers whilst educating the customers about their financial health and affordability. The loans are processed very quickly in order to service the end client needs as soon as possible.

As we are NBFC, we are guided and regulated by RBI. All NBFCs are under operations of RBI and we follow all the rules of the Endeavour we take part in. Suneet Finman is guided by its capable team, working PAN India with a wide array of customer service segments.
